Basic

XIX - The Sun (Moon/Tower): Material happiness, fortunate marriage, contentment. Reversed: The same in a lesser sense.

  • Optimism, always see the bright in life, cherish the birth of creations. An ideal model with sharp minds, creativities and perceptive.
  • May be described as arrogant if imbalance.

XVIII - The Moon (Star, Sun/ x): Hidden enemies, danger, calumny, darkness, terror, deception, error. Reversed: Instability, inconstancy, silence, lesser degrees of deception and error.

  • Living in delusion, high-functioning but unstable. Wild and cunning, unpredictable and always changing which touches the curiosity of mankind.
  • Mischief, luring in the dark, never show the true self.

XVII - The Star (Devil, Moon, Tower/ x): Loss, theft, privation, abandonment, althought some suggest healing, hope and bright prospects in the future. Reversed: Arrogance, impotence, haughtiness.

  • Positivity and aspiration, daydreaming all the time. Open-minded and magnanimous to everything.
  • Naive to the point guillible, believing that everything is on the bright side, leaving a chance for people to take advantage of.

XV - The Devil (Star/ x): Ravage, violence, force, vehemence, ectraordinary efforts, fatality, that which is predestined but not for this reason, evil. Reversed: Evil fatality, weakness, pettiness, blindness.

  • Ambigious, enjoy life as it is the last day. View the world through a sensual lens, will do anything to trade and get what is needed.
  • Living rechlessly to the point of self-sabotage. Addicted to drugs.

XVI - The Tower (Star/ Sun): Misery, distress, ruin, indigence, adversity, calamity, disgrace, deception. Reversed: The same in a lesser degree, oppression, imprisonment, tyranny.

  • See through people's weakness and minds. Extremely indepedence.
  • Sabotage and negativities. Tend to criticise or attack the others, dragging them down. Emotionless and lack of communication skills, often go off limit and damage others.

VI - The Lovers (Sun, Star/ Moon): Attraction, love, beauty, trials overcome. Reversed: Failure, foolish designs.
